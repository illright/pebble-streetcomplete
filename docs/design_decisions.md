# Design decisions

1. The goal of the app is not to replicate all of the functionality of the Android app, but instead provide an enjoyable experience for a subset of the features. This leaves the following out of scope:
   a. Being able to answer questions from a distance — the answering UI will only appear once you arrive at the quest location.
   b. Having a list of nearby quests — the app will show quests one-by-one, and you can skip a quest if you don't want to do it.
   c. Supporting all types of quests — measurement quests require you to use a tape measure or AR ruler, neither of which is practical for a Pebble app.
